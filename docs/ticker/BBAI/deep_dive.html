There's no guarantee they can pivot or execute on new strategies to reverse the trend, especially with a high beta amplifying all market movements.
    3. Extreme Volatility: A Beta of 3.24 means BBAI swings wildly. While this can provide outsized gains on the upside, any general market correction or negative news specific to BBAI could lead to swift and significant losses, wiping out capital quickly.
